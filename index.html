Chicken Square

Select a bird type, age group, and symptom combination to organize what pattern fits best. The result screen ranks possible diagnoses, highlights why they rose to the top, and adjusts for laying status, age, and heavier bird types.

What it does

This version is focused on practical pattern matching rather than generic advice blocks. It compares the selected symptom set against source-backed profiles for common backyard poultry concerns.

Digestive

Flags combinations that fit coccidiosis or crop-related problems.

Respiratory

Raises respiratory disease patterns when sneezing, discharge, noisy breathing, or facial swelling appear together.

Mobility & laying

Separates footpad issues, Marek’s-style neurologic patterns, and reproductive problems in layers.

Symptom triage

Pick the closest match. Results are driven by the combination selected, not by a single symptom alone.

How scoring works

Each possible condition gains points from symptom matches, then receives extra weight from age group and bird type. Stronger symptom clusters rise to the top first.
