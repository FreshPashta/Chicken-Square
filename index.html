Devon's Chicken Squares

A recipe for the best Chicken Squares!

Makes about 8 squares Bake at 350°F Bake time: 35–40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter, plus extra melted butter for brushing
  • 4 cups cubed cooked chicken, preferably chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 to 4 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp chopped chives
  • 2 cans crescent rolls
  • Crushed cornflakes, enough to coat

Instructions

  1. Cream together the cream cheese and 2 tablespoons of butter until smooth.
  2. Mix in the cubed chicken, salt, pepper, 2 to 4 tablespoons of milk, and chopped chives until well combined.
  3. Open the 2 cans of crescent rolls and divide each can into 4 rectangle pieces.
  4. Use your fingers to smooth together the angled perforations so each piece forms a solid rectangle.
  5. Divide the chicken mixture evenly onto the dough pieces.
  6. Pull the 4 corners of each rectangle up to the top, twist to seal, and press the edges together well.
  7. Brush each square with melted butter.
  8. Lift each square with a spatula and place it into crushed cornflakes, coating well by tossing cornflakes over the top and pressing them in.
  9. Place the coated squares on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  10. Bake at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown.

  • Avian Stewardship: A Comprehensive Report on Chicken Home Care, Triage Protocols, and Pathological Management.pdf: Used for brooder temperature guidelines, biosecurity measures, and end-of-life/necropsy protocols.
